Most Networking Is Broken

Mass-market events optimize for footfall, not fit. The signal-to-noise ratio is brutal.

ii

Thirty business cards.
Zero real connections.

Surface-level chatter, no follow-through. Most rooms forget you within 24 hours.

iii
